WHAT IS KEENAN SOCIETY?

Keenan Society is a newly-formed collective established in 2025 in Indonesia.We are starting our journey by focusing exclusively on creating vibrant events in Semarang.

At Keenan Society, our mission is to bring people together through unforgettable nights of Sing Along, Top 40, EDM, Hip-Hop, and RnB.

WHAT CAN WE OFFER?

Music Quality

At Keenan Society, we create a high-energy music experience featuring a dynamic mix of Top 40, EDM, R&B, Hip-Hop, Jersey, Afrobeats, Amapiano, Baile, and Drum n Bass.

Our team ensures every set flows effortlessly, keeping the crowd engaged and the atmosphere electric from start to finish. We aim to deliver exceptional music, vibrant energy, and a night you’ll never forget.

UNFORGETTABLE EXPERIENCE

It’s more than just a party — it’s a journey back to the songs that defined your childhood and the memories that shaped you.

Every night with us is a story waiting to unfold: from the first note to the last drop, the lights, the crowd, and the timeless tracks all come together to create an experience you’ll carry with you long after the night ends.
